# JuegaLink User Guide

JuegaLink is a social platform for finding people to play sports with, organizing pickup games, and connecting with other athletes in your area.

## Getting Started

### How to Create an Account

1. Go to the signup page
2. Enter your username (this will be your unique identifier)
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a password and confirm it
5. Click "Create Account"
6. You'll be logged in and taken to your home feed

### How to Log In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your username
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"
5. You'll be taken to your home feed (FYP)

### How to Log Out

1. Click the profile icon in the navigation bar (person icon)
2. On your profile page, click the "Logout" button

---

## Navigation

The navigation bar appears at the top of every page and has 5 icons:

1. **Home (house icon)** - Takes you to your feed where you see posts from people you follow
2. **Search (magnifying glass)** - Find users or events
3. **Notifications (bell icon)** - View follow requests, joined events, and hosted events
4. **Create Event (plus circle)** - Create a new sports event
5. **Profile (person icon)** - View and edit your profile

---

## Your Feed (FYP - For You Page)

### How to View Your Feed

1. Click the home icon in the navigation bar
2. You'll see posts from people you follow
3. Click on any post card to see the full post with comments

### Why is My Feed Empty?

Your feed shows posts from people you follow. If it's empty:
1. Click the search icon
2. Search for users to follow
3. Click on a user's profile
4. Click the "Follow" button
5. Their posts will now appear in your feed

---

## Posts

### How to Create a Post

1. Go to your profile page (click the person icon)
2. Click the "New Post" button
3. Enter a title for your post (required)
4. Write your content (optional)
5. Optionally select an event to mention from the dropdown (shows events you host or have joined)
6. Click "Create post"
7. Your post will appear on your profile and in your followers' feeds

### How to View a Post

1. Click on any post card in your feed or on a profile
2. You'll see the full post with:
   - Title and content
   - Author information
   - Like button
   - Comments section
   - If the post mentions an event, you'll see the event attendees

### How to Like a Post

1. Open the post by clicking on it
2. Click the heart icon to like it
3. The heart will turn red when liked
4. Click again to unlike

### How to Comment on a Post

1. Open the post by clicking on it
2. Scroll to the comments section
3. Type your comment in the text field
4. Click the "Post" button
5. Your comment will appear below the post

### How to Delete a Post

1. Open your post by clicking on it
2. Click the "Delete Post" button (only visible on your own posts)
3. The post will be removed

---

## Profile

### How to View Your Profile

1. Click the person icon in the navigation bar
2. You'll see your profile with:
   - Your username and profile picture
   - Stats (posts, followers, following)
   - Bio and personal information
   - Two tabs: POSTS and TAGGED

### How to Edit Your Profile

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click the "Edit Profile" button
3. Update any of these fields:
   - Bio (tell others about yourself)
   - Email
   - Age
   - Phone Number
   - City
   - State
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Your profile will be updated immediately

### How to View Your Posts

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click the "POSTS" tab
3. You'll see a grid of all posts you've created

### How to View Posts You're Tagged In

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click the "TAGGED" tab
3. You'll see posts where other users have tagged or mentioned you

### How to View Someone Else's Profile

1. Click on their username anywhere in the app (on a post, in search results, etc.)
2. You'll see their profile with their posts and information

---

## Following & Followers

### How to Follow Someone

1. Go to the user's profile (search for them or click their username)
2. Click the "Follow" button
3. You'll now see their posts in your feed

### How to Unfollow Someone

1. Go to the user's profile
2. Click the "Unfollow" button
3. Their posts will no longer appear in your feed

### How to See Who You Follow

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click on the "following" number in your stats
3. You'll see a list of everyone you follow
4. Click on any username to visit their profile

### How to See Your Followers

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click on the "followers" number in your stats
3. You'll see a list of everyone who follows you

### How to Accept a Follow Request

1. Click the bell icon in the navigation bar
2. Go to the "Follow Requests" tab
3. You'll see pending requests
4. Click "Accept" to allow them to follow you
5. Click "Reject" to decline the request

---

## Events

### How to Create an Event

1. Click the plus circle icon in the navigation bar
2. Fill out the event form:
   - Event name (required) - e.g., "Saturday Basketball at Central Park"
   - Description (optional) - details about the event
   - Date & time (required) - when the event will happen
   - Max players (required) - how many people can join
3. Click "Create event"
4. Your event is now live and others can find and join it

### How to Find Events

1. Click the search icon in the navigation bar
2. Click the "Events" filter tab
3. Type the event name you're looking for
4. Click on an event to see its details

### How to Join an Event

1. Find the event (through search or a post mention)
2. Click on the event to open its details page
3. Click the "Join Event" button
4. You're now attending the event
5. The event will appear in your Notifications under "Joined Events"

### How to Leave an Event

1. Go to the event page
2. Click the "Unjoin Event" button
3. You'll be removed from the attendee list

### How to Delete an Event You Created

1. Go to the event page (you must be the host)
2. Click the "Delete Event" button
3. The event will be removed

### How to See Events You've Joined

1. Click the bell icon in the navigation bar
2. Click the "Joined Events" tab
3. You'll see all events you're attending

### How to See Events You're Hosting

1. Click the bell icon in the navigation bar
2. Click the "Hosted Events" tab
3. You'll see all events you've created

### How to Mention an Event in a Post

1. Create a new post
2. In the "Mention an event" dropdown, select the event
3. The event badge will appear on your post
4. Other users can click the badge to see the event details

---

## Search

### How to Search for Users

1. Click the search icon in the navigation bar
2. Make sure "Users" filter is selected
3. Type a username in the search box
4. Click the search button or press Enter
5. Click on a user to visit their profile

### How to Search for Events

1. Click the search icon in the navigation bar
2. Click the "Events" filter tab
3. Type an event name in the search box
4. Click the search button or press Enter
5. Click on an event to see its details

---

## Notifications

### How to View Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the navigation bar
2. You'll see three tabs:
   - **Follow Requests** - People wanting to follow you
   - **Joined Events** - Events you're attending
   - **Hosted Events** - Events you've created

---

## Tips & Best Practices

### Building Your Network
- Follow people who play the same sports as you
- Join events in your area to meet new players
- Create posts about your sports activities to attract followers

### Creating Great Events
- Use descriptive event names (include the sport and location)
- Add details in the description (skill level, what to bring, etc.)
- Set an appropriate max players limit

### Engaging with the Community
- Like and comment on posts from people you follow
- Tag events in your posts to share experiences
- Keep your profile updated so others can find you

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**
A: Currently, password changes need to be done through account recovery. Contact support for assistance.

**Q: Can I make my profile private?**
A: All profiles are currently public. Your posts are visible to anyone who visits your profile.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support for account deletion requests.

**Q: Why can't I see someone's posts in my feed?**
A: You need to follow them first. Go to their profile and click "Follow".

**Q: How many events can I join?**
A: There's no limit to how many events you can join.

**Q: Can I edit an event after creating it?**
A: Currently, events cannot be edited. You would need to delete and recreate the event.

**Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**
A: Contact support with details about the content you'd like to report.
